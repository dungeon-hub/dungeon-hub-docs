Dungeon Hub Help

/role

Description

Add or remove a role from a user, remove full role groups, and configure per-role behavior. Administrators can also define role requirements that grant roles automatically when players meet certain conditions.

Sub-Commands

  • /role add, /role remove, /role remove-group: Give or remove roles from a user.

  • config set, config reset: Set name schema or the role action for a role.

  • requirements get, requirements list, requirements add, requirements edit, requirements delete: Manage Role Management and Role Requirement Type ✏️.
