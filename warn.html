/warn

Sub-Commands

  • /warn add: Add a warning to a user.

  • /warn deactivate: Deactivate a warning by id, which causes it to not be included in punishment calculations, it will also not be displayed to regular users anymore.

  • /warn list-all: List all warnings a given user ever received, even if deactivated.

  • /warn add-evidence: Add an evidence to a user, in the form of a text or an image.
